About Us

Dawsons Diner was set up in 2016 by Mike and Helen Dawson
They wanted their business to be family run so they then hired brothers, sisters, nieces, and nephews to help.
Currently Mike and Helen are the incredible chefs behind the delicious food.
The current waiters are their daughters Emily and Kate along with their niece Julie and nephew Dylan.
Dawsons Diner makes tasty food, affordable. Always served with kindness and care.
